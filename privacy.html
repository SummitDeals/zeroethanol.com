Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

ZeroEthanol ("we", "our", or "us") is built and operated by Summit Deals LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the ZeroEthanol mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the App:

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google, we store your display name, email address, and profile photo URL in Firebase.
  • Username — A publicly visible username you choose when you create an account.
  • Contribution data — Fuel prices, E0 station tags, and service reports you submit are stored in Firebase and attributed to your account.
  • Submission count — A running count of your contributions, used to determine your rank tier and ad-free eligibility.
  • Device location — Used only to show nearby stations and calculate distances. Your location is never stored on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect precise location history or background location.
  • We do not collect payment information. Any in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google.
  • MPG tracker data and service records are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to our servers.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs or behavioral tracking.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your username and rank on the Leaderboard.
  • To associate submitted prices and station tags with your account.
  • To determine ad-free eligibility based on your contribution count.
  • To process referral rewards when a friend signs up with your code.

Advertising

The App displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and other data to serve relevant ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can remove ads permanently via a one-time in-app purchase or by reaching Elite Fueler status (300 contribution points).

Data Sharing

We are committed to handling your data responsibly. We do not sell your personal information. Station data and prices you submit become part of the crowdsourced database visible to all users of the App.

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and hosting
  • Google Maps Platform — Map display and nearby station discovery
  • Google AdMob — Advertising

Data Retention

Your account data is retained while your account is active. Station contributions remain in the database as part of the community dataset. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at support@zeroethanol.com.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:
support@zeroethanol.com
